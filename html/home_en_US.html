Try HOLLIS beta

HOLLIS is being upgraded in January 2026 and we're offering early access now. This new version of HOLLIS will look very familiar, but includes many technical improvements such as fewer duplicate search results and faster access to new and updated titles. Please note that if you have any Saved Searches in HOLLIS, we will not be able to migrate them and we advise that you recreate them in HOLLIS Beta in 2025 so that they will persist there after the upgrade. We will be migrating your Saved Items (pinned records).

What am I searching?

HOLLIS is the library’s main search tool. The Catalog & Articles search (formerly Everything) searches the catalog plus billions of citations. Or you can choose Library Catalog from the drop-down to limit to Harvard collections. Learn more about searching HOLLIS.
